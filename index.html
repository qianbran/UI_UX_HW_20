Case Study: Letsgo, an AI based Travel Booking App

Users usually have to go through tedious and complicated process to look for the best deals from multiple sources when it comes to travel booking.The APP “Letsgo” uses AI technology to pool useful sources for the user together in one place, and offers the optimal travel booking deals

User Research

five user interviews with potential users. The interviews were conducted either in person or via Zoom, and were recorded and transcribed for further analysis. The entire process took one week, during which time I analyzed the collected answers and generated user personas in order to provide a clearer and more targeted understanding of the user base and needs for the app..

conclusion

AI-assisted technology in price comparing is the key to success, but how to make user trust that Letsgo’s AI has done all the legwork for them is challenging. Building user trust in the AI's capabilities is crucial. Transparency in showcasing the AI's methodology and sources of data can help users understand and trust the results. Providing clear explanations and user-friendly visuals that demonstrate how the AI analyzes and compares prices can also increase user confidence in the technology.
